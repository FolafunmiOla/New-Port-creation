Courses We Offer

Faculties or other centres and Department for Education offers courses across a range of subject areas.

Intermediate

An intermediate entry program is a pathway for students to transition into our university degree program after completing a diploma or certificate program. It provides students with a foundation in their chosen field and prepares them for advanced studies.

Degree

This program is an undergraduate / graduate program that leads to the award of a Bachelor's, Master's, or Doctoral degree. It provides students with in-depth knowledge, skills, and expertise in their chosen field.

Post Graduate

This program is a graduate degree that builds on previous undergraduate study, offering advanced knowledge and specialization in a specific field. Postgraduate programs include Master's and Doctoral degrees.
